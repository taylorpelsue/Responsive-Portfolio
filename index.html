About Me

After growing up in Syracuse, NY, in the summer of 2013 I made my move to Texas in search of new opportunities. Despite major changes, I persevered through countless overwhelming challenges to pursue an Associate's Business degree at Blinn College. After graduating in 2015, I took my customer service skills and began working full- time. It didn't take long before I realized that I wanted more to my life than asking, "This is Taylor, how may I help you?".

I began looking to embrace new skills that apply in a modern, information- age society. I built my first HTML webpage, and immediately felt the sense of belonging I had been looking for. I am now enrolled in the UT Coding Boot Camp, looking forward to eventually seeking employment at a company that boasts regular opportunities to grow.
